About Us

Welcome to moretonisland.com.au where we have your Moreton Island accommodation all sorted in one location, or should we say Moreton Island glamping. Luxury caravans right on the beachfront on the western side of the island. All our caravans are brand new with everything you need to have a fun holiday. A bit like your own resort or hotel room on wheels, situated in the national park, right near the beach.

We’ve been visiting the island now for 40 years on holidays and decided we wanted a change of lifestyle and have moved to the island to live permanently.

Living on an island has it’s challenges and earning a living and keeping busy are the two biggest challenges.

So with barge fares being the same as a car for caravans and so many beautiful camping spots so close to us, our business was born.

With the help of QYAC and Qld National Parks, we are now offering our caravans set up when you arrive.

We both hope to build a regular clientele and meet happy holiday makers on a regular basis.

Don and Sharon Rankin
