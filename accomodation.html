Accommodation

This has to be a first for Mulgumpin ( Moreton Island ). We would like to thank the Quandamooka people who are the traditional owners of this island and also the QPWS for letting us be the first to offer eco caravans For your next holiday.

The vans will be set up when you arrive, nothing to do but relax near the water and campfires are permitted. Just bring your clothes, food and a smile. As we live on the island, we take care of everything to make sure holiday accommodation is the best.

Check out our FAQ page for any questions you have in mind, or feel free to call us on 0417793193.

Fantasy 1 Hybrid Caravan Sleeps 3 Adults + 1 Child

Caravans come supplied with hot and cold water, flush chemical toilet, gas 4 burner stove, kitchen sink, 22″ colour tv, stereo /cd/mp3/usb radio, 95 litre fridge freezer, 240 volt inverter, fans, multiple 12 volt outlets, usb outlets, 240 volt outlets, pillows, quilt, webber bbq, outside table, 4 fold up deck chairs, annex, lots of led lighting, outside wash down shower, cooking utensils, plates, cups and cutlery. King size mattress and 2 small bunk beds. Linen package are included in price
